# Artifact Abilities Reference

A complete reference for every artifact's combat abilities in the Artifacts × Craftics compatibility mod.

---

## Head Slot

### Night Vision Goggles
- **Stat Bonus:** +1 Attack Range

### Superstitious Hat
- 30% chance for bonus loot on kill.
- Adds a duplicate of a random item from the loot pool on loot rolls.

### Villager Hat
- +50% emeralds earned (rounds up to at least +1).

### Cowboy Hat
- On hit, pulls the target 1 tile closer toward you (if the destination tile is in bounds and unoccupied).

### Anglers Hat
- +1 bonus item from loot rolls (duplicates a random item from the existing pool).

### Snorkel
- **Stat Bonus:** +1 Water Power
- Immune to the SOAKED status effect.

### Plastic Drinking Hat
- Heal 1 HP at the start of each turn.

### Novelty Drinking Hat
- *"Am I a Pretty Girl?"*
- At combat start, randomly grants +2 to one of: Melee Power, Speed, Defense, or Luck.

---

## Necklace Slot

### Cross Necklace
- After being hit, the next hit you take deals 50% less damage (minimum 1).
- The shield resets at the start of each turn.

### Flame Pendant
- At the start of each turn, deals 2 fire damage to all adjacent enemies (Manhattan distance ≤ 1) and applies 1 stack of BURNING for 2 turns.

### Thorn Pendant
- When you take damage, reflects 25% (minimum 1) of the damage back to the attacker.

### Panic Necklace
- When you take damage, grants +2 Speed for the rest of the turn.

### Shock Pendant
- 30% chance on hit to chain 3 damage to an enemy adjacent to your target (Manhattan distance ≤ 1).

### Charm of Sinking
- **Stat Bonus:** +1 Defense
- Immune to knockback.

### Charm of Shrinking
- 20% chance to completely dodge any incoming attack.

### Scarf of Invisibility
- **Stat Bonus:** +1 Stealth Range
- Your first attack of combat deals 2× damage.

### Lucky Scarf
- 15% chance to crit for double damage on hit.
- +1 bonus loot item (duplicates a random item from the loot pool).

---

## Ring Slot

### Onion Ring
- **Stat Bonus:** +1 Regen, +1 Max HP
- No active effect (passive stats only).

### Golden Hook
- +2 bonus emeralds per kill (flat addition to all emerald gains).

### Pickaxe Heater
- On hit, applies 1 stack of BURNING for 2 turns to the target.

### Withered Bracelet
- **Stat Bonus:** +1 Armor Pen
- On hit, reduces the target's max HP by 1 (Wither effect).

---

## Hand Slot

### Digging Claws
- On hit, ignores 50% of the target's effective defense (adds the ignored defense value as bonus damage).

### Feral Claws
- Refund 1 AP on kill.

### Power Glove
- **Stat Bonus:** +3 Melee Power
- No active effect (passive stats only).

### Fire Gauntlet
- On hit, applies 2 stacks of BURNING for 3 turns.
- On kill, deals 3 fire damage to all enemies adjacent to the killed target (Manhattan distance ≤ 1) and applies 1 stack of BURNING for 2 turns.

### Pocket Piston
- On hit, knocks the target back 2 tiles away from you.
- If the target collides with a wall or another entity, they take 3 bonus damage.

### Vampiric Glove
- On hit, heals you for 25% of the damage dealt (minimum 1 HP).

---

## Belt Slot

### Crystal Heart
- **Stat Bonus:** +6 Max HP
- No active effect (passive stats only).

### Helium Flamingo
- Immune to knockback.
- Can move through enemies.

### Chorus Totem
- Once per combat: when you would take lethal damage, survive instead. Heals you to 25% of your max HP.

### Obsidian Skull
- Immune to the BURNING status effect.
- 50% fire damage reduction (via blocking the effect application).

### Cloud in a Bottle
- **Stat Bonus:** +2 Speed
- 15% chance to dodge incoming attacks.

### Warp Drive
- Once per combat: on your turn, you can teleport anywhere and attack with unlimited range. The first attack after activation triggers a "Dimensional Strike" message.

### Antidote Vessel
- Immune to POISON and CONFUSION status effects.

### Universal Attractor
- At the start of each turn, pulls all living enemies 1 tile closer toward you (if the destination is in bounds and unoccupied).
- +1 bonus loot item (duplicates a random item from the loot pool).

### Everlasting Beef

### Eternal Steak
- Can use like any other food item, but does not get consumed.
Uses 2 AP and heals the user for 3 HP

---

## Misc Slot

### Umbrella
- Blocks the first hit of combat completely.
- 10% chance to block incoming ranged attacks (attacker range > 1).

---

## Feet Slot

### Bunny Hoppers
- **Stat Bonus:** +1 Speed
- 10% chance to dodge incoming attacks.

### Kitty Slippers
- **Stat Bonus:** +2 Stealth Range
- Enemies ignore you for the first turn (passive, no coded handler).

### Running Shoes
- **Stat Bonus:** +3 Speed
- No active effect (passive stats only).

### Aqua Dashers
- On hit, deals +2 bonus Water damage.
- When you move, applies 1 stack of SLOWNESS for 1 turn to all enemies adjacent to your destination (Manhattan distance ≤ 1).

### Rooted Boots
- At the end of your turn: heal 3 HP if you didn't move, or heal 1 HP if you did.

### Snowshoes
- At the start of each turn, applies 1 stack of SLOWNESS for 1 turn to all adjacent enemies (Manhattan distance ≤ 1).
- When you move, applies 1 stack of SLOWNESS for 1 turn to all enemies adjacent to your destination.

### Steadfast Spikes
- Immune to knockback.
- When hit by a melee attacker (range ≤ 1), deals 2 damage back to them.

### Flippers
- **Stat Bonus:** +2 Water Power, +1 Speed
- On hit, deals +3 bonus damage to SOAKED targets.

### Strider Shoes
- **Stat Bonus:** +1 Speed
- Immune to BURNING and SLOWNESS status effects.

### Whoopee Cushion
- 25% chance at the start of each turn to apply 1 stack of CONFUSION for 1 turn to a random living enemy.
- 15% chance when hit to stun the attacker (they skip their next action). You still take the damage.
