Player Progression

Level up, allocate stats, trade with merchants, and push through New Game+ cycles.

Stats

Defeating a biome boss grants a level-up. Each level-up gives you points to allocate across 8 stats that define your playstyle.

Stat Base Effect
Speed 3 Tiles you can move per turn
AP 3 Action Points per turn
Melee Power 0 Bonus melee damage
Ranged Power 0 Bonus ranged damage
Vitality 0 Bonus max HP
Defense 0 Flat damage reduction
Luck 0 Better loot rolls, crit chance
Resourceful 0 Bonus emerald rewards, cheaper trades

Stats are persistent per player (UUID-based, multiplayer supported). They are displayed in a panel on the inventory screen.

Leveling Up

  • Boss kills grant a level-up.
  • Each level-up gives stat points to allocate.
  • Stats compound with armor trims and equipment bonuses.
  • A stat panel shows current level, all stats, unspent points, and emerald count.

Trader System

There is a 40% chance a trader appears between combat levels when you choose “Continue.”

Trader Specialty Example Items
Weaponsmith Melee weapons Swords, axes (wood→netherite by tier)
Armorer Armor sets Full sets scaling by progression tier
Fletcher Ranged weapons Bows, crossbows, arrows, tipped arrows
Alchemist Potions Strength, Swiftness, Healing, Fire Resistance
Chef Food items Cooked meats, golden apples, golden carrots
Toolsmith Utility items Shields, ender pearls, TNT, fire charges
Curiosity Dealer Rare items Goat horns, enchanted books, trim templates

Trades cost emeralds. Each encounter offers 3–5 random trades. Quality scales with biome tier (1–9).

Victory & Defeat

Victory

When all enemies die, you receive:

  • Mob drops from each enemy
  • Biome loot from a weighted loot table
  • Emerald reward (scaled by the Resourceful stat)
  • Boss kills unlock the next biome(s) + a level-up

After a non-boss victory, choose:

  • Go Home — keep loot, return to hub, reset biome run.
  • Continue — proceed to next level (risk/reward: dying loses run loot).

Defeat

If you die:

  • Inventory cleared — current run items lost.
  • Teleported back to hub — biome run resets (start from level 1).
  • Stats and permanent progress kept.

New Game+

Beat the Ender Dragon in Dragon’s Nest to enter New Game+:

  • All biome progression resets.
  • Stats, emeralds, and gear carry over.
  • Enemies scale +25% per NG+ cycle (HP, attack, defense).
  • NG+ tier is displayed on the level select screen.

Configuration

Over 45 config options are available via Mod Menu. Key categories:

Combat Balance

Enemy HP/ATK/DEF scaling per biome, boss HP multiplier, critical hit chance, ranged accuracy.

Mob Behavior

Passive mob wander chance, predator hunting toggle, enemy animation speed, auto-end turn.

Difficulty

Permadeath mode, heal between levels, enemy range hints, intention preview.

Accessibility

Colorblind mode, larger UI, camera shake toggle.

Economy

Loot multiplier, trader spawn chance, horn drop rate, emerald rewards.

Recommended Builds

A class building guide for each stage of the game. These builds combine armor sets, trim bonuses, weapons, and stat allocations into cohesive strategies.

Early Overworld (Plains → Dark Forest) — “The Survivor”

  • Armor: Leather or Iron (whatever you find)
  • Weapon: Iron Sword (Cleave is very strong early)
  • Stats: Speed +1, Vitality +1 first
  • Strategy: Focus on mobility. Food is your main healing. Save emeralds for the Armorer.

Mid Overworld (Desert/Jungle → River Delta) — “The Rogue”

  • Armor: Chainmail (Sword +2 damage type bonus)
  • Trim: Bolt ×4 (Sword Power +4, full set: crits stun)
  • Weapon: Diamond Sword (Cleave + 30% crit)
  • Stats: Melee Power, Speed, AP
  • Strategy: Crit-stun build. Every crit stuns the target. Cleave hits neighbors. Stack Sharpness enchant.

Alternative Mid Build — “The Sniper”

  • Armor: Leather (Scout, light — invest in Speed)
  • Trim: Sentry ×4 (Ranged Power +4, full set: counter-attack ranged)
  • Weapon: Crossbow (Pierce-through, range 4)
  • Stats: Ranged Power, Speed, AP
  • Strategy: Kite everything. Stay at max range. Pierce-through hits two enemies per shot. Tipped arrows for debuffs.

Late Overworld (Caverns → Deep Dark) — “The Knight”

  • Armor: Diamond (Blunt +2 bonus)
  • Trim: Ward ×4 (Defense +4, full set: 50% less damage stationary)
  • Weapon: Mace (3×3 AoE + knockback)
  • Stats: Defense, Vitality, Melee Power
  • Strategy: Tank build. Stand your ground and smash groups. Ward full-set halves damage when you don’t move. Use potions of Strength for burst.

Nether — “The Infernal”

  • Armor: Netherite (Juggernaut — ALL types +1)
  • Trim: Flow ×4 (Speed +4, full set: kills refund 1 AP)
  • Weapon: Netherite Sword (Cleave + Execute below 30% HP)
  • Stats: Speed, AP, Melee Power
  • Strategy: Speed-kill build. Flow refunds AP on kills. Execute cleans up wounded enemies. Fire Resistance potion is essential.

The End — “The Phantom”

  • Armor: Netherite
  • Trim: Silence ×4 (Stealth +4, full set: invisible 2 turns)
  • Weapon: Netherite Sword + Crossbow (switch between)
  • Stats: AP, Speed, Ranged Power, Melee Power (balanced)
  • Strategy: 2 turns invisible to position. Then burst. Ender pearls for emergency repositioning. Golden Apples for the Dragon fight.

New Game+ (Cycle 2+) — “The Juggernaut”

  • Armor: Netherite
  • Trim: Wild ×4 (Melee Power +4, full set: first attack free each turn)
  • Weapon: Netherite Sword
  • Stats: All-rounder — pump AP and Melee Power, rest into Vitality
  • Strategy: Wild makes your first attack free, giving you effectively +1 AP. Combined with high AP stat, you get 5–6 actions per turn. Stack Golden Apples. Use Goat Horn (Seek) for +3 ATK. Prepare to get wrecked by +50%+ enemies.
