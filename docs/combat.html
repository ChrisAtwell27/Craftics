Combat System

Turn-based tactical combat using Action Points. Every vanilla weapon, armor set, potion, and food item has a role on the battlefield.

Action Points

The player receives 3 AP per turn, modified by stats, potions, and armor trims. Each action costs a certain number of AP. When you run out, your turn ends. Any remaining AP is lost when you end your turn early.

Action AP Cost Description
Move 0 (Speed) Click a highlighted tile. Movement uses the Speed stat (base 3 tiles).
Melee Attack 1–2 AP Click an adjacent enemy. Heavy weapons (axes, mace, crossbow) cost 2 AP.
Ranged Attack 1–2 AP Click an enemy in range with bow (range 3), crossbow (range 4), or trident (range 3).
Use Item 1 AP Eat food, drink a potion, or throw an item.
Block 1 AP Shield in offhand — gain +5 DEF until your next turn.
End Turn 0 AP Press R. Remaining AP is lost.

Damage Calculation

All damage in Craftics follows one unified formula. The result is always at least 1, so no attack is ever completely negated.

Damage = Weapon Attack + Stat Bonuses + Damage Type Bonus − Target Defense (min 1)

Enchantments

Enchantments carry over into tactical combat with turn-based equivalents:

  • Sharpness — adds flat bonus damage to melee attacks.
  • Protection — adds flat bonus defense to the armor piece.
  • Fire Aspect — ignites the target, dealing damage over multiple turns.
  • Infinity — bow does not consume arrows.
  • Thorns — reflects a portion of melee damage back to the attacker.
  • Looting — improves loot drops from defeated enemies.

The Mace has three exclusive enchantments:

  • Density — increases AoE damage.
  • Breach — armor penetration, ignoring a portion of enemy DEF.
  • Wind Burst — increases knockback range on hit.

Shield & Blocking

Equipping a shield in your offhand passively grants +2 DEF at all times. You can also spend 1 AP to brace, raising your shield for a total of +5 DEF that lasts until the start of your next turn. This makes blocking a strong defensive option when you have AP to spare but no favorable attack.
