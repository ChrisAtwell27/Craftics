The Revenant

Plains biome boss — a relentless undead warlord that raises fallen foes to fight again.

The Revenant is an ancient warrior risen from the plains battlefields, bound together by dark energy and relentless spite. It leads a mob of undead and channels supernatural strength into devastating charges.

Abilities

  • Raise the Dead: Summons 1–2 Zombies (6 HP / 2 ATK) every 3 turns. Maximum 3 Zombies alive at once.
  • Death Charge: Charges in a straight line up to 3 tiles dealing ATK+2 damage. Telegraphed 1 turn in advance.
  • Shield Bash: Knocks back an adjacent player 2 tiles for half ATK damage.

Phase 2 — “Undying Rage”

Triggers at ≤ 50% HP. Gains Regeneration (1 HP / turn). Raise the Dead triggers every 2 turns instead of 3. Death Charge damage increases to ATK+4 and leaves a fire trail behind the Revenant.

Strategy

Destroy summoned Zombies to prevent horde buildup. Sidestep charge telegraphs and punish the recovery window.
