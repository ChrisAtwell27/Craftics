The Shulker Architect

End City biome boss — a master builder Shulker that fills the arena with levitating mines and impenetrable shell barriers.

The Shulker Architect has spent countless eons reinforcing the End City, learning to manipulate its shell and surrounding constructs as weapons. Unlike ordinary Shulkers it moves by instantaneous teleportation and expands its shell at will, transforming the arena into a maze of floating hazards.

Abilities

  • Levitation Bolt: Fires a homing bolt that inflicts Levitation on hit, preventing the target from moving for 2 turns. Range of 7 tiles.
  • Shell Seal: Retracts fully into its shell, gaining +8 Defense. While sealed, melee attacks against the Architect deal 2 damage back to the attacker. Lasts 2 turns or until hit by a ranged attack.
  • Construct Deploy: Places 1–2 Shulker Mines at random arena tiles. Mines detonate when a player steps adjacent, launching a Levitation Bolt.

Phase 2 — “Structured Chaos”

Triggers at ≤ 50% HP. Construct Deploy now places 4 Mines simultaneously. Levitation duration extends to 3 turns. Shell Seal now reflects the full ATK value of melee attacks back at the attacker. The Architect teleports to a random location at the start of each of its turns.

Strategy

Prioritize ranged weapons throughout this fight — melee against a sealed Shell incurs heavy self-damage in Phase 2. Destroy Mines proactively by targeting them at range before moving through their trigger zones. A Feather Falling–enchanted boot piece or Slow Falling Potion dramatically reduces the impact of Levitation locks. Map the arena before committing movement.
