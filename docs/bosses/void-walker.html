The Void Walker

Warped Forest biome boss — a reality-shredding predator that tears space apart to strike from anywhere.

The Void Walker is an Enderman transformed by the Warped Forest’s volatile dimensional energies, gaining mastery over spatial rifts and illusory copies. It never stays in one place, striking from behind and vanishing before a counterattack can land.

Abilities

  • Void Rift: Creates 2 portal pairs on empty tiles — stepping onto one teleports you to the other. Lasts 4 turns. Max 2 pairs active.
  • Mirror Image: Summons 2 Enderman clones (8 HP / 3 ATK, take double damage).
  • Phase Strike: Teleports directly behind the player and attacks. Cannot be dodged.
  • Void Pull: Pulls the player 2 tiles toward the boss.

Phase 2 — “Reality Shatter”

Triggers at ≤ 50% HP. Rifts become permanent. Spawns 3 clones. Phase Strike hits twice in succession. Randomly blinks to a new tile at the start of each turn. Void Pull extends to 3 tiles.

Strategy

Destroy Mirror Image clones immediately — they distract and deal real damage. Map rift locations carefully or you’ll teleport directly into the boss.
