The Ashen Warlord

Basalt Deltas biome boss — a wither-blooded commander that permanently scars every foe it strikes.

The Ashen Warlord is a battle-hardened Wither Skeleton that rules the volcanic wasteland of the Basalt Deltas with iron discipline. Every strike it lands permanently erodes its target’s maximum health, and it never enters battle alone.

Abilities

  • Wither Slash: Melee attack that applies a Wither stack, permanently reducing max HP by 3 per hit. Stacks indefinitely.
  • Summon Blaze Guard: Summons 2 Blazes (10 HP / 5 ATK / Range 4) every 4 turns. Cap of 3 active guards.
  • Ash Storm: 5×5 field centered on the player — applies −1 speed and −50% ranged accuracy for 2 turns.
  • Fire Pillar: 4-tile line toward the player dealing 6 damage each. Telegraphed 1 turn in advance.

Phase 2 — “Warlord’s Command”

Triggers at ≤ 50% HP. Gains Speed 4. Wither Slash becomes a 180° arc hitting 3 tiles and applies double Wither stacks. Blaze Guards are replaced by Wither Skeletons (12 HP / 6 ATK, also apply Wither). Fire Pillar becomes an X-pattern.

Strategy

Never let Wither Slash hit repeatedly — the permanent max HP reduction compounds fast. Kill the Blaze Guards immediately, or they whittle you down from range while the Warlord closes in.
