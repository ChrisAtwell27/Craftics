The Hexweaver

Dark Forest biome boss — a master of dark magic who bends curse and vex alike to its will.

The Hexweaver is a rogue Evoker that has delved too deeply into forbidden sorcery, warping its body and mind into something far more dangerous than its kin. It commands swarms of Vexes and layers the battlefield with debilitating curses.

Abilities

  • Vex Swarm: Summons 2 Vexes (3 HP / 2 ATK) every 3 turns. Maximum 4 Vexes alive at once.
  • Fang Line: A 5-tile fang eruption directed toward the player, dealing 4 damage each. Telegraphed 1 turn in advance.
  • Cursed Fog: Projects a 3 × 3 curse cloud at the player’s position — enemies gain +3 DEF and the player loses −2 ATK for 3 turns.
  • Hex Bolt: Ranged attack at up to 4 tiles that applies 1-turn Slowness.

Phase 2 — “Arcane Fury”

Triggers at ≤ 50% HP. Teleports away whenever the player moves adjacent. Vex Swarm now spawns 3 Vexes per cast with a cap of 6. Fang Line erupts in a full cross pattern, firing in both cardinal directions simultaneously.

Strategy

Pressure the Hexweaver into melee before it can lay down Cursed Fog. Kill Vexes quickly or they will overwhelm you while the boss kites.
