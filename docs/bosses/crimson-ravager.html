The Crimson Ravager

Crimson Forest biome boss — a blood-frenzied brute that heals on corrupted ground and breaks through any obstacle.

The Crimson Ravager is a massive Hoglin that has absorbed the pulsing crimson energy of its biome, becoming an unstoppable force of destruction. It crashes through the arena with devastating charges, seeds the field with regenerative terrain, and calls its piglin kin when cornered.

Abilities

  • Gore Charge: 4-tile line charge dealing ATK +3 damage with knockback 3. Destroys obstacles in its path. Telegraphed 1 turn in advance.
  • Fungal Growth: Creates a 3×3 crimson terrain zone centered on itself; boss heals 2 HP per turn while standing on it for 3 turns.
  • Rampage: Hits all 8 adjacent tiles for ATK damage each.
  • Summon Pack: Calls 2 Piglins (8 HP / 4 ATK / Range 3) to the field. Once per fight.

Phase 2 — “Blood Frenzy”

Triggers at ≤ 50% HP. Gains +4 permanent ATK and Speed 4. Gore Charge leaves a fire trail. Rampage extends to a 2-tile radius. Melee attacks no longer knock back.

Strategy

Lure Gore Charge into walls to interrupt it. Stay off the Fungal Growth terrain or the boss heals faster than you can damage it.
