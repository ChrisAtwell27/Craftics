Enemy AI Behaviors

Every mob in Craftics has its own combat intelligence. 40+ mobs with 20+ unique AI strategies.

How AI Works

After the player ends their turn, each enemy acts in sequence. The AI evaluates four possible actions: move, attack, use a special ability, or flee. The decision depends on the mob's registered AI class, which encodes its personality and combat style.

Every mob type has a registered AI class that determines its behavior. When the AI's turn begins it checks line-of-sight, distance to the player, its own HP, and any active status effects before choosing what to do.

Mobs without a registered AI class default to PassiveAI, which means they stand idle and flee when attacked.

Passive mobs won't attack unprovoked. Some can be turned hostile, and a few have unique quirks that make them more than simple bystanders.

Boss: The Warden

The Warden is the first major boss encounter. It hits incredibly hard and becomes far more dangerous as the fight goes on.

Phase 1: 100% to 50% HP

  • Speed 1. Slow but devastating melee damage.
  • Walks directly toward the player each turn.
  • Strategy: Kite and use ranged weapons. Keep your distance and chip away at its massive HP pool.

Phase 2: Below 50% HP

  • Speed increases to 2. Much harder to outrun.
  • Gains Sonic Boom, a ranged attack (range 3) that bypasses armor entirely.
  • Significantly more dangerous. Kiting becomes unreliable.
  • Strategy: Focus on burst damage to finish the fight quickly. Save your strongest abilities for this phase.

Boss: Ender Dragon

The Ender Dragon is the final boss of Craftics. The fight takes place on a special arena grid with end crystals and obsidian towers.

Phase 1: 100% to 50% HP

  • Arena swoops that fly across the grid, damaging every tile in its path.
  • Predictable flight patterns, but the damage area is wide.
  • Strategy: Learn the swoop lanes and position between them. Destroy end crystals to prevent healing.

Phase 2: Below 50% HP

  • Switches to charging directly at the player instead of sweeping lanes.
  • Gains AoE breath attacks that leave lingering damage zones on the grid.
  • More aggressive and harder to dodge.
  • Strategy: Stay mobile. Avoid lingering in breath zones. Use your best burst damage to close out the fight.
